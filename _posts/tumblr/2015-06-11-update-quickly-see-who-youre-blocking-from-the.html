You can now see who you’re blocking on your team and address them directly from the extension, where you’ll be less likely to miss them.

Blockers are tasks that you—or your teammates—are stuck on. You can assign blockers by @mentioning your teammates’ username.

A notification will be sent to the assigned person, where you can see it in the web app—and now the Chrome extension.

Once you’ve helped your teammate, you can cross the blocker off in the web app or your extension.  

Have a question about blockers? We’re happy to help. :)
