Have you ever felt demotivated when tasks on your to do list become stale over time? Or that you’re not sure what to do when they are stale?

Now, you can see when stale tasks need your attention on Dayboard. We want to help you overcome these issues as soon as they come up.

The notifications are shown whenever you have tasks more than four days old, followed by a warning sign. However, if you are using Dayboard in a team, and you’ve got blockers mentioning you by your teammates, that would come up first before this one.

This doesn’t mean you shouldn’t pay attention to them, but you might want to help out your teammates first, or at least let them know you’ll get to them soon.

Simply click into the notification to see how you can resolve them. Login to see it in action.

We hope this is helpful in helping you achieve more. Love to know your feedback!

For now, this feature only works in the web app. Stay tuned on our Twitter @DayboardCo for more updates soon.
