We’re very excited to announce: Dayboard now works with Slack!

With the new integration, any updates from your team can be automatically sent to a custom Slack channel. This helps your entire team stay in the loop, even if they’re not logged in to Dayboard. Give it a try today!

To add integrations to your organization, you must be the account owner. Please tweet us or email us at support [at] dayboard.co if you need help!

Love,

Your friends at Dayboard

**What is Dayboard? **Dayboard’s a minimalistic task management app that helps you focus on your 5 most important tasks each day. We built a nifty Chrome extension that replaces your new tabs page and we help you sync your team’s individual task lists so you can quickly catch up with your team.

P.S. Not on Slack? We’ll be adding more integrations in the future and would love your feedback for what you’d like to see next. Let us know!
